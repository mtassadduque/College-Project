About the college

Since 1959, the Government College of Technology in Samanabad has supplied Faisalabad's textile mills, engineering workshops and construction firms with diploma-qualified technologists. Labs are built around the machines students will actually operate on the job, and every term ends with an industry placement review rather than a written exam alone.

The college is a public institution under the Punjab Board of Technical Education, and admission is open each year to matriculation-level students across the district.
